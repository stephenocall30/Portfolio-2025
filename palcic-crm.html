Palcic Taekwondo CRM

A custom CRM to manage students, billing, grading, and reporting.

Context & Problem

Manual spreadsheets and paper forms slowed down admin and created errors. The goal was a centralised tool for student records, payments, and reporting.

Objectives & Constraints

  • Fast add/update for student records
  • Automated payment reminders
  • Reports for attendance, payments, grading

Constraints: modest budget, simple hosting, non‑technical staff.

Process

Discover

Interviews with staff; mapped workflows; minimum viable dataset from spreadsheets.

Design

Wireframes for student list, detail, add student, payments, reporting; tested navigation and terms.

Deliver

Responsive web app with role‑based access; email/SMS reminders; exportable PDF reports.

Key Decisions & Trade‑offs

“Limit advanced reporting in MVP to keep core features rock‑solid.”

Outcomes & Impact

  • Registration time 10 → 3 minutes
  • Overdue accounts down 25% in Q1
  • Clear grading readiness reports

Reflections & Next Steps

Add QR attendance and a parent portal for billing and grading updates.
